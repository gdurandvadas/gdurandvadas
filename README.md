# Hi

I'm a Platform Product Engineer. I build internal products and services that
sit between developers and the platform, with a strong focus on user
experience, clear goals, and product signals that help shape what comes next.

What I enjoy most is turning policies, process, and general annoyance into
something people can actually use with a low entry bar. The work is usually a
mix of research, feature design, technical implementation, stakeholder
alignment, and continuous iteration through feedback, feature requests, and
metrics.

I'm defined by a mix of logic and creativity. I can spend hours refining a
deeply structured spreadsheet with the same focus I bring to spending hours in
a sequencer getting lost in rhythm. That is also how I like to build products:
understand the system, break the problem apart, and make the result feel
simple for the people using it.
